Our Approach

Signal Resolution builds GTM intelligence systems for B2B SaaS founders. Our core product is not the diagnostic — the diagnostic is free. What you pay for is the intelligence layer: the Signal Correction Report, the ICP Resolution Framework, the Archer workspace, and the done-with-you or done-for-you system build. Because our paid deliverables are either personalized to your session data or involve committed advisory time, our refund structure reflects the nature of each product type.

We stand behind the quality of our work. If something went wrong technically — your report wasn't delivered, was corrupted, or contained a material error — we will make it right, no questions asked. Contact us within 7 days of purchase.

When We Will Refund

We will issue a full refund if your report was not delivered within 24 hours of purchase, contained a material technical error, or you were charged more than once due to a payment processing error. Email rome@signalresolution.com within 7 days of purchase with your order number and a description of the issue.

04 —

When We Do Not Refund

We do not issue refunds if you changed your mind after receiving your report, disagreed with the diagnostic result or recommendations, experienced results different from examples in marketing materials, or provided inaccurate information during the diagnostic that affected your result.

05 —

How to Request a Refund

Email rome@signalresolution.com with subject line Refund Request — [your order number], the email used at checkout, and a brief description of the issue. We respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds appear on your statement within 5–10 business days.
