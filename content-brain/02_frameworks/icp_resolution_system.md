# ICP Resolution System — signal resolution

## What It Is
A system for identifying WHO to target and WHAT STATE they are in before messaging or acquisition.

---

## Core Idea
Most companies describe their market.

Very few resolve it.

---

## The Problem
Traditional ICP:
- Industry
- Company size
- Job title

This tells you who they are.

It does NOT tell you:
- If they are ready
- If they feel urgency
- If they will convert

---

## The Solution
The ICP Resolution System uses layered logic:

1. Firmographic Gate
2. Psychographic Signals
3. Technographic Context
4. Behavioral Timing

---

## How It Works

### Step 1 — Firmographic Gate
Filter companies that can actually buy.

### Step 2 — Psychographic Resolution
Identify buyer state using real language.

### Step 3 — State-Segment Mapping
Group similar states together.

### Step 4 — Target High-Resolution Segments
Focus only on segments with urgency and clarity.

---

## Key Insight
Markets don’t buy.

States buy.

---

## What Makes This Different
- Language-first, not assumption-first
- State-based, not static segmentation
- Filters before targeting
- Built for real execution, not theory

---

## When to Use
- When conversion is inconsistent
- When CAC is rising
- When targeting feels unclear
- When messaging “should work” but doesn’t

---

## What It Replaces
- Generic ICP templates
- Persona guessing
- Broad targeting strategies
