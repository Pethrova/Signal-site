# ICP — signal resolution

## Target Audience
B2B SaaS founders (Seed to Series A)

---

## Company Profile
- Early traction to scaling stage
- Small to mid-sized teams
- Founder-led growth or early marketing hire
- Limited clarity on ICP or messaging

---

## Core Problems
- "We don’t know who this is really for"
- "We get traction but it's inconsistent"
- "CAC is rising and we don’t know why"
- "We’re targeting broadly but converting poorly"
- "We tried personas but they don’t work"

---

## Emotional State
- Confused
- Frustrated
- Slightly overwhelmed
- Under pressure from growth expectations
- Unsure what’s actually wrong

---

## What They Want
- Clear ICP
- Predictable conversion
- Lower CAC
- Faster sales cycles
- Confidence in targeting

---

## What They Believe (Wrong)
- ICP is just company size/industry
- More traffic = more growth
- Messaging is the main issue
- Sales team is the bottleneck

---

## What’s Actually True
- Their ICP is not resolved
- Their audience is mixed in state
- They are targeting without filtering
- They are describing, not resolving

---

## Language They Use
- “Nothing is converting”
- “We need better leads”
- “We’re getting traffic but no pipeline”
- “Our messaging isn’t landing”
- “We don’t know who to target”

---

## Content Goal

Make them realize:
1. Their problem is not what they think
2. There is a deeper layer (resolution)
3. There is a system to fix it
