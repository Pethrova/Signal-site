## Queue Rules

1. Fill keyword, stage, content type, and framework before starting any generation
2. High priority items are generated first — always
3. Maximum 1 data report per week (time-intensive)
4. At least 1 diagnostic content piece per week (highest conversion type)
5. All website articles go to /09_drafts/website/ before LinkedIn/Medium adaptation
