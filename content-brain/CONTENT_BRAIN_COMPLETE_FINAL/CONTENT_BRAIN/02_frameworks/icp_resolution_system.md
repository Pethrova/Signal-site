## Core Idea
Most B2B SaaS companies describe their market.

Very few resolve it.

Description tells you who someone is.
Resolution tells you what state they are in.

Only state drives conversion.

---

## The Problem With Traditional ICP

Standard ICP frameworks return:
- Industry
- Company size
- Job title

This is firmographic description. It answers:

"Could they buy?"

It does NOT answer:

"Will they buy?"
"Are they ready now?"
"What is driving their urgency?"

Signal without resolution is noise.

---

## The Four-Layer Hierarchy (Hierarchical Determinism Model™)

The ICP Resolution System processes targeting decisions in strict sequence. Each layer gates the next.

### Layer 1 — Firmographic Gate™
Binary pass/fail filter.

Questions answered:
- Does the company meet minimum revenue threshold?
- Is the team size consistent with buying capacity?
- Is the motion (sales-led, founder-led) the right fit?

If they fail Layer 1, psychographics are irrelevant. Stop here.

### Layer 2 — Technographic Context
Tool choices as admissions.

Questions answered:
- What tools are they using?
- What does their stack reveal about their execution maturity?
- Are they using tools that signal misalignment vs. readiness?

AI-era note: Traditional technographic signals are degrading. AI agents are abstracting integration pain. The new signal is AI adoption posture × execution velocity.

### Layer 3 — Psychographic Signal (Psychographic Resolution™)
Buyer state identified through real language, not personas.

Questions answered:
- What language are they using publicly?
- What are they complaining about?
- What are they searching for?
- What is their internal urgency level?

This is where Market State Signals™ are extracted and classified.

### Layer 4 — Behavioral Timing
Trigger events and urgency detection.

Questions answered:
- Has something recently changed for them?
- Are there observable trigger events (new hire, funding round, product pivot)?
- Is their urgency rising or falling?
