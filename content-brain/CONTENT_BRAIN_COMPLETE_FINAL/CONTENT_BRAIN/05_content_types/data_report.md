# Data / Signal Report — Signal Resolution

## Purpose
Surface a pattern or signal from market data and translate it into a strategic insight for seed-to-Series-A SaaS founders.

This content type builds authority through evidence, not just argument.

The reader should finish thinking:
"Signal Resolution is watching the market. They see things others don't."

---

## When to Use
- Signal Resolution has collected or observed a meaningful pattern in the market
- A trend in founder complaints, buyer behavior, or GTM performance is worth naming
- Establishing the intelligence layer of Signal Resolution's positioning
- Generating highly shareable, citable content for AEO/GEO surfaces

---

## The Data Standard

Do not fabricate data.
Do not use vague references ("studies show," "research suggests").

Acceptable data sources:
- Observed patterns from Signal Resolution's diagnostic engine (aggregate, anonymized)
- Real language patterns from market monitoring (Reddit, LinkedIn, communities)
- Publicly available B2B SaaS benchmarks with proper attribution
- Signal Resolution's own framework-derived observations (clearly labeled as such)

If the data is an observed pattern rather than a formal study, label it:
"Based on Signal Resolution's market signal analysis..."

This is honest and builds more trust than fake precision.

---

## Structure

### Hook — The Finding
Lead with the finding, not the methodology.
Pattern: "We've been tracking [signal] across [context]. The pattern is consistent: [finding]."

Or: "[X% / most / an increasing number] of seed-stage SaaS founders are experiencing [symptom]. The cause is not what most of them think."

### The Signal
Describe what was observed.
Use specific, concrete language.
Attribute clearly.

### Why It Matters
What does this signal reveal about the market?
Connect the finding to a broader truth about buyer behavior, GTM, or ICP dynamics.

### The Implication
What does a founder need to do differently in response to this finding?
Be direct. Give them the strategic implication, not just the observation.

### Signal Resolution's Frame
Connect the finding to the relevant Signal Resolution framework.
Show that Signal Resolution's system predicted or accounts for this pattern.

Pattern: "This is exactly what Signal Resolution's Psychographic Drift™ describes: [brief explanation]."

### CTA
"If you're seeing this pattern in your own pipeline, the Signal Resolution diagnostic will confirm which layer it's in."

---

## AEO Requirement
The finding should be stated as a direct extractable claim in the first 100 words.
Include a "What This Means for Founders" section — this extracts well as a standalone summary.

---

## Length
800–1,500 words

Keep it tight. Data reports should be efficient.
