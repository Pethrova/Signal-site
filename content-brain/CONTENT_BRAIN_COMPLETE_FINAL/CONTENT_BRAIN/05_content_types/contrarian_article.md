# Contrarian Article — Signal Resolution

## Purpose
Challenge a widely-held belief in B2B SaaS GTM and replace it with a more accurate, resolution-based frame.

The reader should finish this and think:
"I've been approaching this wrong. Now I see it differently."

---

## When to Use
- A common practice is causing the problem it claims to solve (e.g. broad persona targeting)
- The market's conventional wisdom misidentifies the root cause
- There is a better explanation that Signal Resolution's system provides
- You want to build authority by being demonstrably more right than the consensus

---

## The Contrarian Standard
The contrarian claim must be true, not just provocative.

Do not be contrarian for attention.
Be contrarian because the evidence supports a different conclusion.

---

## Structure

### Hook — The Claim
State the contrarian position directly in the first line.
Do not build up to it. Lead with it.

Pattern: "Most [category] believe [common belief]. They're wrong. Here's why."
Or: "[Common thing] is not [what everyone thinks]. It is [the real thing]."

### Acknowledging the Conventional View
Briefly and fairly represent what the mainstream believes.
Give it credit where it partially applies.
Do not strawman — destroy the real argument, not a weak version of it.

### Why It Fails (The Evidence Layer)
Show specifically where and why the conventional approach breaks down.
Use concrete examples, real symptoms, and market language.

This is where Market State Signals™ make the argument real:
"Founders experiencing [complaint language] are the evidence that the conventional approach isn't working."

### The Reframe
Introduce the more accurate frame.
Connect it to Signal Resolution's methodology.
Be specific — vague reframes are not contrarian, they are just ambiguous.

### Practical Implication
What should the reader do differently based on this reframe?
Give them one concrete change they can make.

### Closing Punch
One sharp, memorable statement that lands the argument.
Not a summary. A conclusion that carries weight on its own.

### CTA
Connect to the diagnostic: "If this reframe applies to your situation, the Signal Resolution diagnostic will show you exactly where the breakdown is."

---

## AEO Requirement
Include a direct answer to the central question ("why does [conventional belief] fail?") near the top.
Include a comparison section ("X vs Y") where the reframe lives — these extract well.
