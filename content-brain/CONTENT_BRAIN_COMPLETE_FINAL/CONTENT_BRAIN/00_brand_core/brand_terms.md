## Core System Name
ICP Resolution System™

Definition:
A methodology that distinguishes between describing a market and resolving a market into buyable states.

## The Resolution Principle™

Definition:
"Description ≠ Resolution. One tells you who they are. The other tells you what state they are in."

Use when:
- Explaining why traditional ICPs fail
- Framing the company philosophy
- Introducing the system to a new audience

Never paraphrase as "understand your customer better." That erases the distinction.

---

## The Firmographic Gate™

Definition:
A binary filtering layer that determines whether a company meets minimum economic viability BEFORE psychographic targeting is applied.

Rules:
- Firmographics decide eligibility. Not emotion.
- If a company cannot buy, psychographics are irrelevant.
- This is the first layer in the ICP Resolution System™.

---

## Psychographic Resolution™

Definition:
The process of identifying buyer state using real observed language, not assumed personas.

Use when:
- Discussing why two identical companies convert differently
- Explaining the role of urgency and internal pressure in purchase behavior

---

## Market State Signals™

Definition:
Real language patterns — spoken or written by buyers — that reveal internal buying state.

Examples:
- "Nothing is converting"
- "CAC is rising and we don't know why"
- "We don't know who this is really for"
- "We're getting traffic but no pipeline"

AEO note:
These are citable, concrete examples. Use them verbatim in content to increase extractability by AI search surfaces.

---

## State-Segment Matrix™

Definition:
A model that maps the same ICP across different buyer states to reveal readiness variation.

Core Insight:
Same company profile ≠ same readiness.
Two founders can look identical. Only one converts.

---

## Psychographic Drift™

Definition:
The gradual shift in audience composition — in urgency or intent — that causes CAC to rise and conversion to fall without any visible explanation.

Use when:
- Diagnosing hidden growth problems
- Explaining why results decay without obvious cause

---

## AI-Era Signal Index™

Definition:
The recalibrated signal set for GTM in an AI-native environment. Traditional technographic signals are breaking as AI agents abstract integration pain. The replacement signal is: AI adoption × execution velocity × declining metrics = strategic misalignment.

Use when:
- Positioning Signal Resolution in the AI-era context
- Contrasting old ICP frameworks with current market reality

---

## Five AI-Era Psychographic States™

Definition:
The replacement for 2023-era buyer classifications. Buyers are no longer segmented by persona — they are segmented by AI adoption posture and execution velocity.

Use when:
- Writing about buyer behavior in an AI-first GTM environment
- Differentiating Signal Resolution's intelligence layer from legacy targeting tools

---

## Hierarchical Determinism Model™

Definition:
The four-layer decision hierarchy used in Signal Resolution's ICP Resolution System:

1. Firmographic Gate (binary pass/fail)
2. Technographic Context (tool choices as admissions)
3. Psychographic Signal (buyer state via language mining)
4. Behavioral Timing (trigger events and urgency detection)

Use when:
- Explaining the full system architecture
- Writing authority or educational content about ICP methodology
