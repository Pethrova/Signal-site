# ICP Primary — Signal Resolution

## Primary Segment
Seed-stage B2B SaaS founders
$500K–$3M ARR
10–40 employees
Founder-led, sales-led motion

## Secondary Segment
Series A B2B SaaS (GTM expansion stage)
$3M–$10M ARR
Early GTM hire or VP Sales in seat
Beginning to systematize acquisition

---

## Company Profile (Primary)
- Founder is still directly involved in sales
- Growth is happening but feels inconsistent
- No dedicated marketing function yet, or early marketing hire
- ICP has been "defined" but not tested against real signal
- Messaging was written from assumptions, not from buyer language

---

## Core Problems (In Their Words)
- "We don't know who this is really for"
- "We get traction but it's inconsistent"
- "CAC is rising and we don't know why"
- "We're targeting broadly but converting poorly"
- "We tried personas but they don't work"
- "Our messaging should work — but it doesn't"
- "We have some customers but we don't know why they bought"

---

## Emotional State
- Confused about root cause
- Frustrated that working harder isn't fixing it
- Slightly overwhelmed by conflicting GTM advice
- Under pressure from growth expectations (investors, co-founders, self)
- Unsure whether the problem is the product, the message, or the market

---

## What They Want
- Clear ICP they can actually act on
- Predictable, repeatable conversion
- Lower CAC
- Faster sales cycles
- Confidence when making targeting decisions

---

## What They Believe (Wrong)
- ICP is defined by company size and industry
- More traffic or leads will fix conversion
- Messaging is the primary bottleneck
- Sales is underperforming
- They just need a better funnel

---

## What's Actually True
- Their ICP is unresolved — described, not resolved
- Their audience contains mixed buyer states
- They are targeting based on profile, not state
- They are spending on signal-blind acquisition

---

## Language They Use (Raw Market Signal)
- "Nothing is converting"
- "We need better leads"
- "We're getting traffic but no pipeline"
- "Our messaging isn't landing"
- "We don't know who to target"
- "We have customers but they're all different"
- "We're not sure who our best customers are"
- "CAC keeps going up"
- "Sales cycles are getting longer"

This language is the raw input for Market State Signals™ analysis.

---

## Content Goal

Every piece of content should move the reader through 3 realizations:

1. The problem is not what they think it is
2. There is a deeper layer — resolution, not description
3. Signal Resolution has a system to fix it

---

## Buying Resistance

Primary objections before buying:
- "We already have an ICP"
- "We're not ready for this yet"
- "This feels like more complexity"
- "We need leads first, then we'll fix targeting"

These are all symptoms of the same thing: they are describing the problem without resolving it.
