# Language Bank — Signal Resolution

## What This Is
A swipe file of real language from the market — the exact words, phrases, and complaints that B2B SaaS founders use when they are experiencing the problems Signal Resolution solves.

This file is the tone anchor for all content generation.

AI should pull from this, not invent language.

---

## Raw Founder Complaints (High Signal)

### On ICP Confusion
- "We don't know who this is really for"
- "We have customers but they're all so different"
- "Our ICP keeps changing"
- "We defined our ICP but sales still feels like guessing"
- "We tried personas but no one uses them"
- "We target everyone and convert no one"

### On CAC and Conversion
- "CAC keeps going up and we don't know why"
- "We're getting leads but nothing is closing"
- "Traffic is up but pipeline is flat"
- "Our close rate is inconsistent"
- "We can't figure out which deals will close"
- "The deals we win, we can't explain"
- "We're spending more to get the same result"

### On Messaging
- "Our messaging should work but it doesn't"
- "We A/B tested everything and nothing moved"
- "The pitch lands with some people and not others"
- "We don't know what to say on the homepage"
- "We wrote the copy three times and it's still not converting"

### On GTM Confusion
- "We don't have a repeatable sales motion"
- "Every rep is doing something different"
- "Marketing and sales are not aligned"
- "We need to systematize this"
- "We're not sure where to focus"

### On Investor Pressure
- "We need to show better unit economics before the next round"
- "CAC has to come down or the model doesn't work"
- "We need to prove the GTM works at scale"
- "We're burning too much on acquisition"

## Emotional Language (Psychographic State Indicators)

### Urgency Indicators (High Readiness)
- "This has to work"
- "We're running out of time"
- "Nothing we're trying is working"
- "We need to fix this now"
- "We can't keep burning at this rate"

### Confusion Indicators (Resolution Needed)
- "We're not sure"
- "We don't know"
- "We can't figure out"
- "We've tried everything"
- "We keep going in circles"

### Blame Indicators (Wrong Attribution — Opportunity)
- "Our sales team isn't closing"
- "Marketing is sending bad leads"
- "The market isn't ready"
- "We need better creative"

When a founder is blaming the wrong layer, they are a high-fit buyer who hasn't found Signal Resolution yet.

- "We need a shared ICP definition across the team"
- "Marketing qualified leads aren't converting in sales"

## Language to Mirror in Content

When writing content, use phrases from this bank.

Do NOT:
- Paraphrase into corporate language
- Use softer, more "polished" versions

Do:
- Use the raw language as hooks
- Quote the exact phrases in content
- Let the reader see themselves in the words

Example hook pattern:
"If you've ever said 'our messaging should work but it doesn't' — the problem is not the message."
