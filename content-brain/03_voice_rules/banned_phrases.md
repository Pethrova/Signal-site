# Banned Phrases — Signal resolution

Avoid using these words and phrases:

- unlock
- leverage
- delve into
- ever-evolving
- landscape
- game-changer
- cutting-edge
- in today’s market
- seamless
- optimize (overused context)
- maximize
- transform your business
- next-level
- powerful solution
- robust strategy
- innovative approach

---

## Why

These phrases:
- sound generic
- reduce credibility
- signal AI-generated content
- weaken authority

---

## Replacement Rule

Instead of:
"Leverage this powerful strategy"

Say:
"Use this approach"

---

Instead of:
"In today’s fast-paced landscape"

Say:
"Right now"

---

## Final Rule

If it sounds like marketing copy → remove it

If it sounds like a real founder would say it → keep it
