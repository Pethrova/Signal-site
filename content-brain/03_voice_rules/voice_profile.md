# Voice Profile — signal resolution

## Tone
- Direct
- Clear
- Practical
- Structured
- Insightful
- Occasionally contrarian

---

## Style Rules
- Short sentences
- One idea at a time
- No fluff
- No filler transitions
- No “trying to sound smart”

---

## Writing Feel
- Sounds like explaining to a smart founder
- Grounded, not theoretical
- Calm but sharp
- Not hype
- Not motivational fluff

---

## Sentence Style
- Mix short and medium sentences
- Avoid long complex sentences
- Prefer clarity over cleverness

---

## What to Avoid
- Buzzwords
- Corporate tone
- Over-explaining
- Vague statements

---

## Goal of Writing
- Make the reader think
- Make the reader realize something new
- Make the reader question their current approach

---

## Editing Layer (Human)
When reviewing:
- Strengthen the hook
- Increase tension
- Add sharper insight
- Remove anything generic
